About

I design surveys, manage field research, and analyze data for policy-relevant projects. My experience spans Africa, MENA, and Europe, with work in Kenya, Rwanda, Uganda, Mozambique, Lebanon, Palestine, Italy, and Portugal.

I currently work as a Research Assistant at Yale Economic Growth Center. My work includes survey design, data analysis, and research support on development-focused projects. In my master's thesis, I tested a novel method to measure technology adoption under the supervision of Professor Catia Batista.

I have also worked on the midline evaluation of the Enhancing Quality and Inclusive Education project in Mozambique, Lebanon, and Palestine, and on Digital Citizenship Education across Council of Europe member states.
