Artisanal Mines and Women Empowerment

                       
Solo-authored
                                               
                            Draft coming soon                        
                                               

                            This paper examines the impact of artisanal mining operations on women's economic empowerment                             in sub-Saharan Africa. Using novel survey data and GIS mapping, we analyze how proximity to                             mining sites affects women's labor force participation, income generation, and decision-making                             power within households.

Can Games Measure Mobile Money Adoption? Evidence from Mozambique

                       
Solo-authored
                                               

                            I combine tools from behavioral economics and mobile money transaction data to test a novel                             method for measuring mobile money adoption. This study explores whether behavioral games can                             serve as effective instruments for understanding financial technology uptake patterns in                             developing economies.
